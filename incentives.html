Georgia – Perfect Destination for Incentive Travel

The numerous UNESCO sites, museum and religious treasures, impressive rock cut monasteries and cave towns as well as millennia old archaeological sites and remote areas make Georgia as well as the whole Caucasus an attractive destination for travelers interested in culture. Here you come across the profound evidence of a flourishing culture, which is rich in cultural sites and museums.
