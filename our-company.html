Little of History We, group of friends, mountaineers, people of different professions, but united by love to mountains, in late 80’s decided to create organization to promote mountaineering and apply it to practical, even commercial projects…. and Georgian Guide Service (GGS) was born. Organizing expeditions, taking part in rescue operations, exchanging visits with mountaineers from other countries was main activity of GGS. Later little metamorphose brought name extension - Georgian Guide Service - Caucasian Travel, as sign of intention starting travel business, even not much was happening, in terms of tourism. However, in 1991 we decided to be more tourism dedicated and encouraged with an idea to promote our beloved country Georgia, its Culture, Nature, History, Traditions, People to just opening world. It was the time when Soviet Union was “passing away” and Country was making first independent steps. Introducing Georgia and Caucasus to the world became main mission of Company, fully dedicated to safety, quality services, sincere hospitality, creativity and rock-solid reliability. We keep same commitment and enthusiasm for 30 years, welcoming many thousand visitors from all over the world to give them unforgettable experience framed in excellent services.
